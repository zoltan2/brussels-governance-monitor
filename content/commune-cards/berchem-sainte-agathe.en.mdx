## A stable coalition renewed

Berchem-Sainte-Agathe is a small municipality of 25,800 inhabitants led by Christian Lamouline (Les Engagés). The Engagés-PS-Ecolo coalition was renewed for the 2024-2030 term with 21/29 seats.

## Transparency: 5 out of 6 criteria

The **budget** and council **minutes** (via the EDITORIA platform) are published online, the council is broadcast via **livestream** on YouTube (channel 1082berchem), the **mandate registry** is accessible, and the municipality has a dedicated **citizen participation** platform. No **Open Data** portal.

## Citizen participation: dedicated platform

Berchem-Sainte-Agathe has a digital participation platform (particip.berchem.brussels) inviting citizens to co-build the municipality's future.
