## Brussels' participation pioneer

Woluwe-Saint-Lambert has approximately 61,000 inhabitants. In 2008, it became the **first Brussels municipality** to adopt a citizen participation code. Olivier Maingain (DéFI) leads the municipality with Les Engagés.

## Transparency: 5 out of 6 criteria

The **budget** and council **minutes** (dedicated page organised by year) are published online, the council has been **livestreamed** since 2019, the **mandate registry** is accessible, and the municipality runs a **participatory budget** of EUR 200,000/year with 60 to 80 annual meetings. No dedicated **Open Data** portal.

## Citizen participation: a model

Woluwe-Saint-Lambert devotes **EUR 200,000 per year** to its participatory budget, the highest among Brussels municipalities. The 2008 participation code structures 60 to 80 citizen meetings per year.
