## The largest municipality by area

Uccle is the largest municipality in the Brussels Region by area (22.9 km²) and the fifth by population (nearly 87,000 inhabitants). A residential and green municipality, it is governed by a renewed MR-DéFI + Ecolo-Groen coalition.

## Transparency: 5 out of 6 criteria

Uccle achieves a very good score. The **budget** and council **minutes** (with dedicated archives) are published online, the council is broadcast via **livestream** on YouTube, the **mandate registry** is accessible, and the municipality runs a **participatory budget** of EUR 150,000 via the MonOpinion platform. Only the **Open Data** portal is missing.

## Citizen participation: MonOpinion

Uccle uses the **MonOpinion** platform (based on Decidim) for its participatory budget of **EUR 150,000**, allowing citizens to propose and vote on improvement projects for the municipality.

## Recent developments

The **property tax** (PRI) has been raised from 2,940 to **3,400 additional centimes**. The **PFAS** contamination at the Sicli site (Chaussee de Neerstalle 430) remains a major issue: a 100-metre safety perimeter has been in place since September 2025 (see PFAS dossier). A collaboration project with the municipality of **Linkebeek** is under consideration.

## Tram 82 extended to the Dieweg (7 June 2026)

From **Saturday 7 June 2026**, tram 82 runs again via **rue de Stalle** and **square Marlow** and is **extended to the Dieweg**, following works on rue de l'Etoile. Uccle is now directly connected to **Brussels-Midi station**.

Source: [BX1 (7 June 2026)](https://bx1.be/categories/news/uccle-le-tram-82-est-desormais-prolonge-jusquau-dieweg/).

## CetteFamille — first shared housing for elderly in Belgium (June 2026)

The first **shared housing for elderly people in Belgium** has opened in Uccle. This model, developed by **CetteFamille**, combines autonomy with home care support, as an alternative to traditional care homes. **Iriscare** management visited the facility, signalling institutional interest in this model within the Region's elderly care policy.

Source: [Iriscare (3 June 2026)](https://www.iriscare.brussels/fr/2026/06/03/cettefamille-simplante-en-belgique-iriscare-a-visite-la-premiere-maison-a-uccle/).
