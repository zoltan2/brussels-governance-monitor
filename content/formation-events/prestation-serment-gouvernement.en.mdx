## Swearing-in ceremony

The Brussels Parliament convened on Saturday 14 February at 8am for the swearing-in of the new regional government.

**Boris Dilliès** (MR), Mayor of Uccle since 2017, was designated Minister-President by MR party president Georges-Louis Bouchez.

## Government composition

### Ministers
- **Boris Dilliès** (MR) — Minister-President: Security, Urban Planning, Tourism, Cleanliness, Energy, Research
- **Ahmed Laaouej** (PS) — Minister: Housing, Local Authorities, Equal Opportunities
- **Laurent Hublet** (Les Engagés) — Minister: Economy, Employment, Digital Economy
- **Elke Van den Brandt** (Groen) — Minister: Mobility, Public Works, Road Safety, Animal Welfare
- **Dirk De Smedt** (Anders) — Minister: Budget, Finance, Civil Service, Digital Transition

### Secretaries of State
- **Ans Persoons** (Vooruit) — Environment, Climate, Urban Renovation, Heritage
- **Audrey Henry** (MR) — External Relations, SIAMU, Bicultural Affairs
- **Karine Lalieux** (PS) — Social Affairs, Health (Cocom)

### Parliamentary support
- **Benjamin Dalle** (CD&V) — Parliament Vice-President, VGC President

## Next steps

- **23 February**: reading of the Regional Policy Declaration
- **25 February**: confidence vote in the Brussels Parliament
