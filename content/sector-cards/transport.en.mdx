## Metro 3 under the North Station: compromised (23 April 2026 hearing)

The 18th hearing of the Brussels Parliament's Special Metro 3 Commission (23 April 2026) heard **Henri Ernst**, CEO of Galère SRL (SM Progrès consortium appointed by Beliris): "We assume there is little chance the project [under the North Station] will proceed." Beliris has refocused the consortium's mission on surface urban planning around the station — no longer the underground cavity.

A new **V.E.R.T. technique** would require **+€75.119M** on top of the €22.56M already spent, with no feasibility guarantee. The site has been idle for several years and SM Progrès teams have left. The northern branch of Metro 3 (Bordet via tunnel connected to the North Station cavity) is suspended beyond even the 10-year freeze set in the RPD.

See the cross-cutting dossier: [Metro 3](/en/dossiers/metro-3).

Sources: [BRUZZ Urbanism](https://www.bruzz.be/actua/stedenbouw/topman-bouwfirma-kans-klein-dat-metro-3-project-onder-noordstation-er-komt-2026-04-23), [La DH Bruxelles](https://www.dhnet.be/regions/bruxelles/bruxelles-mobilite/2026/04/23/le-chantier-du-metro-3-sous-la-gare-du-nord-a-larret-pour-plusieurs-annees-KVXPKWRUEZAO5GXOX6OIHNQIQI/) (23 April 2026).

## Infrabel: €57.8M for 7 accessible stations (24 April 2026)

The federal Council of Ministers approved on 24 April 2026 an amendment to Infrabel's 2023-2032 multi-year plan: **€57.8M** to raise platforms and improve access at the 7 stations **Brussels-South (Midi), Etterbeek, Schaerbeek, Haren-South, Uccle-Stalle, Forest-East, Bordet**. Funding from postponing Rail Ghent-Terneuzen and other accessibility projects (starting 2030+).

Sources: [BRUZZ](https://www.bruzz.be/actua/mobiliteit/van-etterbeek-tot-vorst-oost-zeven-brusselse-stations-worden-toegankelijker-2026-04-24), [BX1](https://bx1.be/categories/mobilite/infrabel-lance-de-nouveaux-projets-ferroviaires-57-millions-deuros-prevus-pour-les-gares-bruxelloises/) (24 April 2026).

## STIB — Brussels-South: tram interruption for 1 year (from 27 April 2026)

STIB worksite **from 27 April 2026 to end April 2027** in the tunnel under Place Bara (trams 4 and 10). Reason: replacement of a 40-year-old concrete slab. Impact: tram 4 interrupted between Brussels-South and Porte de Hal (rerouted via line 82 to Wiels); tram 10 suspended between Brussels-South and Churchill; tram 51 at new terminus Lemonnier; tram 81 affected. Alternatives: metro 2/6, bus 48, walking (5 min Brussels-South – Porte de Hal).

Source: [La Libre](https://www.lalibre.be/belgique/mobilite/2026/04/23/les-trams-interrompus-pendant-un-an-a-la-gare-du-midi-voici-ce-quil-faut-savoir-BCX2T6UU3ZCIZD5EANVNYXV3CM/) (23 April 2026).

## STIB — end of Taxibus PRM minibus service (2027)

On 17 March 2026, the Mobility Minister confirmed in a Brussels Parliament committee the **non-renewal of the 12-minibus Taxibus fleet** and the transition to **private taxis** by **late 2027**. The minibuses will be removed by 2028 (LEZ non-compliance). Estimated cost for users: **€2 → €5-8 per trip**. The PRM grant rises from €4.7 to **€6.7M/year** (+€2M), but the renewal cost (€4.5M) is deemed unfundable.

See the dossier: [PRM accessibility](/en/dossiers/accessibilite-pmr).

## Brussels Airport — CAE closes pilot training centre (April 2026)

Canadian aviation training company **CAE** has announced the closure of its pilot training centre at Brussels Airport (Steenokkerzeel) by the end of 2026. **22 jobs out of 27** are at risk. Legal consultation procedure started on 8 April 2026. Reason: cost structure exceeding revenue, outdated simulators, declining demand.

Source: [BRUZZ Economy](https://www.bruzz.be/actua/economie/trainingscentrum-voor-piloten-verdwijnt-aan-brussels-airport-22-jobs-bedreigd-2026-04-21) (21 April 2026).

## Coalition agreement: structuring decisions (recap)

- **Metro 3 frozen for 10 years** in favour of a tram completing the loop
- **New Regional Mobility Plan** in preparation (Good Move evaluation completed end 2026, new plan in force 2030)
- **Tram 15** (Brussels-North – Tour & Taxis) maintained
- **LEZ maintained**: annual pass €350 (social €200), Euro 5 diesel fines postponed (no earlier than July 2026)
- **STIB fares** preferential rates for youth/seniors maintained
- **Bollards** removed
- **2nd car-free Sunday** per year

## Shared mobility

The Region has approximately **15,700 shared free-floating vehicles** (e-scooters and bikes). The Villo! concession (360 stations) expires in September 2026 with no identified successor. **Bolt** launched **1,200 new e-scooters** "Bolt7" in Brussels in April 2026.
