## 2nd reading vote

On **Tuesday 21 April 2026**, the **Chamber of Representatives' Interior Committee** approved, in **second reading**, the bills relating to the merger of the six Brussels police zones.

**Vote:** 9 for (Arizona majority) / 3 against (PS + PVDA) / 3 abstentions (Vlaams Belang + Groen-Ecolo).

The second reading had been requested by the PS after the first-reading approval on 4 March. It made it possible to integrate a set of **amendments**, notably based on a Chamber legal note.

## Next steps

- The text moves to the **plenary Chamber**, the last step before promulgation.
- Once the law is published in the Belgian Official Journal, Brussels will have **at most 18 months** to merge the six zones into a single one.
- The bill also contains **voluntary merger** incentives for the rest of the country.
