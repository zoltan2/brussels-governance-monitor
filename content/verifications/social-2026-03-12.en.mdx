## Verification performed on 12 March 2026

**Result: factual error corrected.**

### Trigger

Report by a reader of the Spanish digest: the ratio 8,000 / 185,700 (4.3%) did not match Brussels' share of national exclusions.

### Error identified

The Social card stated "approximately 8,000 people affected in Brussels". This figure comes from the official website of the **municipality of Brussels-City** (bruxelles.be, 4 November 2025), which covers only one of the 19 municipalities of the Region (~190,000 inhabitants out of 1.2 million).

### Verified figures for the Brussels-Capital Region

| Source | Regional figure | Scope |
|--------|----------------|-------|
| Actiris | ~42,000 | Cumulative Jan. 2026 – Jul. 2027 |
| ONEM / La Libre | ~37,000 | 22.1% of 168,063 national |
| Brulocalis / RTBF | 45,000 | < 55 years, > 2 years unemployed |
| Vivalis (CBSS) | 24,700 | < 55 years only |

### Correction applied

- Social card ×4 locales: "8,000" → "~42,000 (Actiris estimate, Brussels-Capital Region)"
- Digest w10 ×10 languages: same correction
- Clarification added: "in the Brussels-Capital Region" instead of "in Brussels" (disambiguation)

### Cross-content coherence

The Employment card already contained the correct figure of 42,000 (source Actiris) since February 2026. The inconsistency between the two cards had not been detected during previous audits.
