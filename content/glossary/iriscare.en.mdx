Iriscare is the executive arm of the Joint Community Commission (COCOM/GGC) for health and social welfare matters. Created in 2019 following the 6th reform of the Belgian State, it manages accreditations for care facilities, funds nursing homes and outpatient mental health services, and coordinates Brussels family allowances through Famiris.
