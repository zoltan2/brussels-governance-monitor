## Budget 2026 approval

The Finance Committee of the Brussels Parliament approved the 2026 regional budget draft on Monday 23 March 2026. It is the first full budget passed since the 613-day institutional crisis, which had forced the Region to operate under provisional twelfths.

## Key figures

- **Revenue**: €6.622 billion
- **Expenditure**: €7.613 billion
- **Deficit**: €957 million
- **DPR commitment**: deficit below one billion — target met

The deficit partly relies on the so-called "code 8" mechanism, which excludes approximately €1 billion in strategic participations from the ESA calculation. Validation of this mechanism by the NAI (National Accounts Institute) is expected in April 2026.

## Next steps

- **Friday 27 March** — Plenary vote in the Brussels Parliament
- **April 2026** — NAI validation of "code 8"
