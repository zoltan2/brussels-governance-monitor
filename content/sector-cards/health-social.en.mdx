## Government agreement: announced impacts

According to concordant press sources, the agreement of 12 February 2026 includes several measures that affect the bi-communal health and social sector:

- **Administrative reform (merger of pillars)**: the merger of 25 regional bodies into 3 to 4 entities could affect the governance of COCOM and Iriscare. The precise terms are not yet known
- **Regional drugs commissioner**: this new role, announced under the security chapter, intersects with COCOM's competences in harm reduction and addiction care
- **Budgetary effort**: the trajectory to return to balance by 2029 implies an expenditure review that also concerns COCOM's budgets, which are already operating on provisional twelfths

**Key consideration**: the end of the caretaker period makes it possible to unblock accreditations, Iriscare agreements and the structural plan to combat homelessness. The scale of this relaunch will depend on upcoming budgetary arbitrations.

## Federal and regional developments (February 2026)

### INAMI budget 2026: 41.3 billion EUR

The INAMI General Council approved the 2026 health care budget at **41.297 billion EUR**, an increase of **1.566 billion** corresponding to the 2% growth norm + indexation. This budget covers:

- 150M EUR in medical services management
- 50M EUR for hospitals
- 228M EUR in pharmaceutical savings
- 47M EUR in savings from other providers

Hospitals benefit from improved cash flow: 580M EUR in accelerated payments in 2025, 275M EUR expected in 2026.

Source: Le Specialiste, "le budget des soins de sante 2026 approuve par le Conseil general de l'INAMI".

### e-Health plan 2026-2029

The FPS Public Health published a new **e-Health action plan** covering the period 2026-2029, focused on the digital management of health data. This plan applies to the entire territory, including Brussels.

Source: FPS Public Health, e-Health action plan 2026-2029.

### European Disability Card

Iriscare issued **5,871 European Disability Cards** (EDC) to Brussels residents in 2025 (announced on 10 February 2026). This card facilitates access to services and discounts throughout the EU.

Source: Iriscare, press release of 10 February 2026.
