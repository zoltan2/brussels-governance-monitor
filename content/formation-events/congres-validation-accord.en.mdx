## Thursday, February 12 in the evening: four parties approve

In the hours following the announcement of the government agreement, four of the seven coalition parties held membership congresses:

- **MR**: **unanimous** approval, by show of hands and using "YES" cards. The congress was held at Albert Hall (Etterbeek) starting at 8:30 PM.
- **Les Engagés**: **almost unanimous** approval (one vote against). The congress was held at the Belgian Comic Strip Center.
- **Anders**: **unanimous** approval by the party board and the Brussels regional congress.
- **CD&V**: **unanimous** approval by the Brussels party council.

## Friday, February 13: three remaining congresses

Three parties still need to convene their members to validate the agreement:

- **PS**: membership congress scheduled for 6 PM
- **Vooruit**: membership congress scheduled during the day
- **Groen**: congress scheduled during the day

## Next step

If the three remaining congresses approve the agreement, the swearing-in ceremony of the new Brussels government is scheduled for **Saturday, February 14**.
