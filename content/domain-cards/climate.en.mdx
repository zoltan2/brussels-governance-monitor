## Coalition Agreement: Announced Commitments
The agreement of 12 February 2026 sets environmental measures but **does not define new climate targets**:

- **LEZ maintained**: the schedule for future phases is unchanged
- **Renolution replaced** by **zero-interest loans** (EUR 200M) — impact on energy renovation to be assessed
- **Brownfield sites preserved**: Wiels, Ajvil, Donderberg protected; 18-month moratorium on 4 other sites
- **Vivaqua-Hydria merger**: water management consolidated, recapitalisation of **EUR 180 million** (Region at 49.99%)
- **EUR 30 million** for the water merger transition

**Ambiguous signal**: the removal of Renolution (replaced by loans) and the absence of new CO2 targets do not allow the climate trajectory to be considered unblocked. The status remains `delayed` pending details on energy transition financing.
