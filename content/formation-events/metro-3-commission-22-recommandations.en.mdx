## Eight months of inquiry, 22 recommendations

After **8 months** of work and more than **50 hearings**, the Brussels Parliament's special committee on the Metro 3 governance failures, chaired by **Anne-Charlotte d'Ursel** (MR), finalised and adopted its findings and **22 recommendations** on **Thursday 9 July 2026**.

The recommendations are grouped into **6 axes**: governance, quality of studies and environmental impact, management of technical risks and budgetary sustainability, legal framework and financing, local impact, and prerequisites before completing the Metro 3 project. Key measures include a single, accountable governance structure within one administration, with a project director accountable before the government and Parliament, an early budget alert mechanism, a guarantee of independent technical studies, mandatory complete geotechnical and heritage studies before any construction site, and a single window with compensation mechanisms for local residents.

The report is still to go to a **vote in plenary session of the Brussels Parliament on 17 July 2026**.
